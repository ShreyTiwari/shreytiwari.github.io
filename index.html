About Me

Hey!

I'm a software engineering PhD student in the Software and Societal Systems Department at Carnegie Mellon University. I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering from PES University in 2020.

My research interests lie in the domain of Software Reliability and Cloud Computing. I have interned at Microsoft Research India, where I was advised by Dr. Akash Lal as part of the Cloud Reliability team. Prior to that, I was a software engineer at Citrix Systems, working with the VPN Gateway Solutions team.
